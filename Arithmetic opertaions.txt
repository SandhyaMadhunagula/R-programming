Print all the arithematic operations
Write a R program that calculates and prints the results of arithmetic operations such as addition, subtraction, division, and multiplication between the numbers 
8 and 4.
Sample 1:
Input:   Output:12
                4
                2
                32
Solution:
cat(8+4,"\n")

cat(8-4,"\n")

cat(8/4,"\n")

cat(8*4,"\n")
